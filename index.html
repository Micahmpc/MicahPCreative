A little about me...

Designer and strategist from Seattle, Washington. I found a passion for art as a youngster drawing in the gutters of my notebooks, and floating from medium to medium as a I grew up. This in turn led me to pursue design in college, where I earned my degree and graduated with honors in 2009. I also earned a degree in business and marketing which taught me how to think strategically and analyze data in search of creating the best possible product.

My professional experience followed suit from my youngster years, building and moving as I created my arsenal of knowledge and skills. I have multiple years experience in graphic design, visual design, web development, and UX design.

My goal is to guide and build products and solutions that make a positive difference in peoples lives and make the world an easier place.

My other goal is to quit my day job, open up a woodshop where I make furniture 3 days a week, and feed my family by fishing the other 3. Sunday is for Seahawks. Kawkaaww!

A Little About Me...

Hi I’m Micah! I’m a designer and thinker with a knack for strategy and solving problems. My background includes designing user experiences, web interfaces and full sites, branding, and marketing among other things. In my free time I pursue hobbies like woodworking in my home shop, and fishing. The draw for me to design and also to my hobbies (other than awesome furniture and delicious food), is that they all involve a good deal of challenge that requires learning and strategizing, and ultimately learning from failure in some cases. I think the best feeling in the world is conquering a challenge. And through design, I'm able to work my way through challenge until I achieve the desired outcome. Success.
